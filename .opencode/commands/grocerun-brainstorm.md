## Instructions

1. Treat brainstorm as interactive. Ask clarifying questions when needed.
2. Review relevant project status, ADRs, coding rules, and technical designs before recommending an approach.
3. Create or update `planning/brainstorm/YYYY-MM-DDTHHMM_<topic-slug>.md`.
4. Capture options, trade-offs, open questions, and whether the idea should be promoted.
5. Stop at user decision gates; do not silently choose when direction is ambiguous.
